Apple Dapple Cake

Description:

This apple dapple cake recipe is moist and flavorful, studded with apple chunks, raisins, coconut, and walnuts.

Submitted by irenedee | Updated on December 1, 2025

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 3 large eggs
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups peeled, diced apple
  • 1 cup flaked coconut
  • 1 cup raisings
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10-inch tube pan.
  2. Combine sugar, oil, and softened butter in a large bowl for the cake; beat with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. Mix in eggs, one at a time; mix in vanilla. Gradually add flour, baking soda, and salt; stir until well combined. Fold in apples, coconut, raisins, and walnuts. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean, about 90 minutes. Let cool in the pan for a few minutes. Run a knife around edges to loosen; invert carefully onto a serving plate or cooling rack.
